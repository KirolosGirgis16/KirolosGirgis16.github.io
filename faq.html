Frequently Asked Questions


1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First you need to go the HZ Printer Page You can print a document with your Personal Student Card. You have to request the Student pass but after that you have access to the printer. You also need this student card for the exams later on this year.


2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

You can find the instructions for the Scanner on the HZ Scanner Page.


3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

You can use this link for The HZ Webshop.


4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

You can find all the usefull information about the Covid-19 situation on This Website.

5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book your project space on This Website. You should see when a project room is available and for how long.

6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You have to reveal your HZ Student Card at the Gate. After that you have access to the Parking lot
