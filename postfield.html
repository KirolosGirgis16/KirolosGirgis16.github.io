IT Consultant


I chose to research this job because a brother of a friend of mine is a IT Consultant. He also went to this school and he followed the same study program. He works at DON Bureau for a few years now and told me it is a very challenging and fun job. He also told me that the transition from the study to your job is very smooth.

Troughout your study programme you have to choose between Software engineer, Business IT Consultant and Data science. He chose for Data science track.

After i searched more about this job it maybe something for me in the future, I will keep it in my mind for sure.
